Mission Statement

  1. The goal of Altus InSite is to increase the investment appeal, credibility and performance of commercial real estate by creating quality processes that establish current market and relative performance data that are:
    • National and Consistent
    • Accurate and Timely
    • Credible, Transparent and Independent
  2. To accelerate the differentiation of commercial real estate offerings and services by establishing credible criteria and processes that identify, measure, improve, recognize and reward the performance of buildings, owners, managers, occupants and their advisors, suppliers and service providers.

Market Data and Perspective

Office Market

Current and historical data on all 5,200 existing office buildings in 6 major markets and 7 tier two markets across Canada along with all of the buildings which are currently Under Construction or in Pre-Leasing Mode. Powerful search and reporting tools allow users to access data and dynamic graphs related to Tenant Options, Available & Vacant Space, Occupancy Costs, Leasing Transactions, Changes in Occupied Area and more.

Retail Market

Current details on over 3,600 retail properties segmented into one of 10 Retail Formats, with details on approximately 59,000 stores sorted by 31 Categories, 6 Groups and 4 Types. Multiple search and reporting tools allow users to access data and dynamic graphs (coming soon) related to inventory and breakdowns by Format, Categories, Groups and Types, as well as data and visuals related to Market Share and Tenant Mix Analysis. Street retail is not covered.

Industrial Market

Provides coverage of more than 20,000 Light Industrial properties in Calgary (2,741), Ottawa (1,670) and Toronto (16,540), with Edmonton (3,584) launching in February. We are also continuing with our national expansion and Vancouver, Montreal and Quebec City are currently in development. Calgary launched in November 2008, is fully operational and is now live. Toronto launched in November 2007 and went live in April 2008.

Investment Trends Survey

Altus InSite, with assistance from key personnel at Altus Group undertake a survey of the Canadian real estate industry to determine the informed consensus opinion on investment performance trends and valuation parameters from Canada's key investors, lenders and other opinion leaders. The results of this quarterly survey are presented online as a series of data reports and colour charts and address Office, Retail, Industrial and Multiple Unit Residential asset classes for 7 major markets across Canada.
